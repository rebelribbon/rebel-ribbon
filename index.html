Our Cookies

At Rebel Ribbon, every cookie is a masterpiece, crafted with passion and precision. We believe that the finest creations begin with the finest ingredients. That's why we use only premium, locally-sourced butter, pure cane sugar, and the richest vanilla to ensure each bite is a taste of perfection.

Our cookies are made in small batches to preserve their delicate flavors and tender crumb. Hand-cut and meticulously decorated, each piece is an edible work of art, designed to make your moments unforgettable. Whether it's a celebration or just because, our cookies bring a touch of sweetness and elegance to every occasion.

From the mixing bowl to your doorstep, we pour our hearts into every step, ensuring the highest quality and the most delightful experience. Taste the difference that love and artistry can make.

The Magic Behind the Dough

Rebel Ribbon didn’t just happen—it was baked from pure passion and a little bit of magic. In 2017, Alinka Chalker decided to turn her daydreams into dough, transforming simple cookies into edible works of art. Fast forward to 2025, her sister Tabata jumped in, bringing even more creativity (and maybe a little sisterly mischief) to the mix. Together, they’ve been crafting cookies that don’t just look good—they steal the show.

At Rebel Ribbon, we don’t believe in ordinary. Our cookies are all about making statements and turning heads. Whether it’s a birthday bash, a wedding, or just a Wednesday that needs some sweetness, we’re here to elevate your moment. Hand-drawn designs, custom details, and the finest ingredients come together to create something that’s one-of-a-kind, just like you.

Why do we do it? Because life’s too short for boring cookies. We’re here to make your celebrations unforgettable, one bite at a time. From the first sketch to the last sprinkle, we pour our hearts (and a bit of sass) into every order. At Rebel Ribbon, we don’t just make cookies—we create memories that stick around long after the crumbs are gone.

How should I store my cookies?

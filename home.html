Our Story

Rebel Ribbon began as a passion project, transforming simple cookies into edible works of art. Founded by Alinka Chalker in 2017 and later joined by her sister Tabata in 2025, our brand blends craftsmanship, creativity, and heartfelt design.

Every cookie we create is more than just a treat—it's a personalized touch for life's special moments, from birthdays to weddings and beyond.

What We Offer

Every cookie we create is a blend of flavor and artistry, handcrafted to add a personalized touch to your special moments. From custom designs to timeless classics, each bite is made with care and precision.

Why Choose Us?

We go beyond baking—we create edible experiences. With premium ingredients, meticulous designs, and a passion for craftsmanship, Rebel Ribbon ensures that every cookie is as meaningful as the occasion it celebrates.

The Magic Behind the Dough

In 2017, Alinka turned her passion for baking and art into a creative outlet, founding Alinka's Custom Cookies. What started as a small, home-based business quickly became known for its intricate designs and commitment to quality. In 2025, her sister Tabata joined, and together they rebranded as Rebel Ribbon, combining their creativity and sisterly bond into edible works of art.

As a woman-owned small business, Rebel Ribbon is built on the belief that the smallest details make the biggest impact. From birthdays to weddings, we bring artistry and purpose to every cookie, meticulously designed and beautifully packaged to elevate any occasion.
